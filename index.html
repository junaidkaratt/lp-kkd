Frequently asked questions

Is food included?

+

Yes — breakfast, lunch and dinner are included with every room type at Koodu Hostel.

Do you have separate hostels for Men and Ladies?

+

Yes, we maintain fully separate accommodation blocks and entrances for Men and Ladies, each with CCTV surveillance.

Is WiFi available?

+

Yes, high-speed WiFi is available throughout the hostel, ideal for remote work and online classes.

Can working professionals stay?

+

Absolutely — Koodu is home to a mix of students, interns, freshers and working professionals from Infopark, SmartCity and CSEZ.

Is bike parking available?

+

Yes, secure on-site bike parking is available free of cost for all residents.

+

Can I move in immediately?

+

Yes, immediate occupancy is available — move in today with a simple booking process.
